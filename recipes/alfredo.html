Chicken Alfredo

Description:

A nice play on chicken Alfredo, easy to make and yummy to eat. My entire family enjoys it, even my 1-year-old! This dish is extremely creamy and delicious with the spiced chicken! You'll love it for sure!

Ingredients:

  • Noodles
  • Sauce
  • Pepper

Here are the directions!

  1. Boil Noodles
  2. Heat up sauce
  3. Mix together and enjoy!
