Chicago Deep Dish Pepperoni Pizza

Description:

This deep dish pizza is amazing! You'll love the pepperoni taste!

Ingredients:

  • Pizza Crust
  • Sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Cheese

Here are the directions!

  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Spread sauce on pizza crust
  3. Add cheese and pepperoni
  4. Bake for 20 - 25 minutes
